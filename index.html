Coliving; What is it?

Let's Aim High

Coliving is for people who want a environment that actively supports them in living with purpose and intention.

Who makes us up?

Professionals, entrepreneurs, artists. People committed to building a better world.

Why?

We seek excellence, believing that our environments can be used to inspire and empower the people that live there. The serendipity of our day-to-day interactions humanizes - which is why we want urban - and allows us to have stronger relationships than in just the digital world that we spend so much time in.

Rebuilding community, one landmark building at a time.

Our group brings generations of historic preservation to the table

Landmarks are built from a passion for excellence, and our passion is undoubted. From creating mixed commercial-residential spaces that require almost no heating and cooling - Passivhaus  without the regulated overhead of a certification - to historic preservation expertise in buildings all over the District, we can make a project bloom. 

Sustainability enacted

Coliving houses enable sustainable lifestyles through sharing and efficient use of resources and space.  A large shared kitchen means more group meals, less duplication of resources, and groups which are commited to supporting each other.
